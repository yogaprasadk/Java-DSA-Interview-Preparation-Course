matrix data structure is two dimension array arranged in rows and columns
It is commonly used to represent mathematical matrices.
it is used in various fields like mathematics,computer graphics and data processing.
matrix is otherwise known as 2DArray or grid
representation of a matrix:
 the cell a[0][0] is the first element of the first row and first column.

            columns-->
rows        00 01 02
|           10 11 12
|           20 21 22
